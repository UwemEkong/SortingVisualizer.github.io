How Does Quick Sort Work?

Quick sort is a Divide and Conquer algorithm. It first picks a pivot element (usually the first or last element), and partitions the array around the selected pivot. When we partition, we first put our pivot in its proper position in the sorted array, and then we put all smaller elements before our partition, and all larger elements after our partition.

Best Case: O(nlog(n))
