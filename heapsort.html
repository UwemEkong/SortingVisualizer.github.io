How Does Heap Sort Work?

Heap sort is essentially an imporved version of selection sort. While sorting, we divide our data into two regions: sorted and unsorted. As we iterate, we grab the largest element from the unsorted portion and place it into the sorted region.

Best Case: O(nlog(n))
